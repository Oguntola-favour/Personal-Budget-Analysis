## About the project
A full year personal budget tracker and analysis built in Microsoft Excel. 
This project tracks monthly expenses across 15 categories, identifies 
spending patterns, and visualises trends across the year.

## Tools used
- Microsoft Excel (pivot tables, charts, cross-sheet formulas)

## Files
- `budget_data` tab — raw monthly expense data across all categories
- `dashboard` tab — summary metrics, charts and insights

## Key findings
- Total annual spend was £21,286 against an annual income of £26,400
- Rent was the single largest expense at £7,200/year — 30% of total income
- February was the highest spending month at £2,625 due to a spike in online purchases
- Food, groceries and restaurants combined averaged £300/month
- Black Tax totalled approximately £890 annually — a real and often invisible cost that most budget templates ignore
- After paying myself £300/month, leftover fun money averaged £74.50/month
